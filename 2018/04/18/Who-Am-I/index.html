Who Am I?

Posted on 2018-04-18 | Visitors:

This is ChangQing (Wallace).
For me, writing blog is not an enjoyable thing to do after a long day of work.
Especially if it’s not about gamings or sports.
It will be about tech and ideas, as well as stuff I learn from other sources.
I will call this place my notebook.

However, like Mark Twain said

“I didn’t have time to write a short letter, so I wrote a long one instead.”

so to future me: if you find these notes cumbersome and painful to read, please excuse my poor writing skills.

Oh, who am I? I am a software developer, living in Boston.
I also like blockchain technology and Vitalik Buterin.
SpaceX is probably the coolest company at the time I am writing this.
But none of these really matters.
